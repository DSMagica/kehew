Meet Kehew Construction

Family Owned and Operated For Decades

Kehew Construction is a family owned and operated company that has been providing home building and civil construction services for over 40 years. In addition to residential single family and multi-family projects, we also provide a wide array of civil services such as excavating, pipeline construction, right-of-way clearing, road construction, and pad construction.
